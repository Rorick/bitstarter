Dance fever is what brings dancers together. Professionals and amateurs meet each other online, share their dance videos, thoughts, opinions and comments about that dances and other topics, follows new performances of their favorites, listen to best music for your dance, track dance events of interest, watch dance schools and clubs, existing and new, disco places and so on and so forth. But that is just social part. Professional dancers are able to start their online dance classes, or individual trainings. And amateurs are able to choose trainers that most fit them. But how to teach dance via internet? By whatching trainer-provided video, and by submitting your own video to trainer for analysis. And of cause by live communication.

Dance fever allows you to find dance mates, teachers or students depending on what you need. In several points, dance fever is:

  • full-blown social platform targeted to dancers
  • e-learning platform targeted to dancers
  • video analysis suite again target to dancers
  • source of your favourite dance music - yes, it is targeted to you, dancer!

FAQ

I always dreamed to try dancing. How Dance Fever can help me?
Dance fever can do a couple of things for you. You can find like-minded persons, find and enter relevant groups, find appropriate dance schools, performances and so on. And last, but not least, you can find your personal online trainer, that will guide you to your dance Olymp by communicating with you, providing you with relevant videos and checking your progress by your videos, with support of advanced video analysis tools, that allow to mark good and bad parts right inside the video and store that data.
Do I need to pay for using Dance Fever?
No. But you may pay for services from teachers, dance schools and so on. Usually Dance Fever gets a small percent of these sums for bringing you together.
